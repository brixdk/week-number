1. Description of Service

Week Number is a Chrome browser extension that provides the following functionality:

  • Displays the current week number in the browser toolbar
  • Shows a monthly calendar view with week numbers
  • Optionally integrates with Google Calendar to display your calendar events
  • Provides customization options for theme, week start day, and date/time formats

The Service is provided free of charge and is designed to enhance your productivity by providing quick access to week numbers and calendar information.
