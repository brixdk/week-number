Privacy Policy for Week Number

Last updated: November 12, 2025

1. Overview

Week Number is a Chrome extension designed to integrate with your Google Calendar to only read events and give a conveniently calendar overview. This Privacy Policy explains how the extension handles your data and your privacy rights.

2. Data access and usage

The extension requests read-only access to your Google Calendar through Google's secure OAuth 2.0 authorization system. This access allows Week Number to:

  • Read calendar events to determine and display week numbers accurately.
  • Read your Google Calendar settings (such as start of the week and time format preferences) to match your configuration.

Week Number does not and cannot modify, delete, or create any events in your calendar.

3. Data storage

The extension stores only minimal settings locally in your browser (e.g., your preferred start of the week and 12/24-hour format). This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any external server.

4. Data sharing

Week Number does not share, sell, or transmit any user data to third parties. All data access is performed securely and solely within the context of your Chrome browser.

5. Security

Week Number uses Google’s official OAuth 2.0 authentication flow, ensuring that your credentials are never handled or stored by the extension. Data is processed using secure Chrome Extension APIs.

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Updates will be published on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
